Budget Travel Tips

How to Travel on a Budget

  • Book flights early to get the best deals.
  • Travel during off-season for cheaper rates.
  • Stay in hostels or budget accommodations.
  • Use public transportation instead of taxis.
  • Eat like a local — avoid tourist restaurants.
  • Look for free walking tours or local events.
  • Use travel apps to find discounts on food and attractions.
